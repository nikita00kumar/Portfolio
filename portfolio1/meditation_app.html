Breathe Easy

App Concept

For this App concept, I used a minimalistic aesthetic.

I used a light blue and a warm toned yellow, as they

are opposite each other in the color wheel.

The slight contrast with the dark and light greys

create a less straining view for users.

The app concept design is very simple and

understandable so users can navigate through it easily.
